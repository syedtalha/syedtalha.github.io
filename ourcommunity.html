Who we are

A board of trustees for the Trust/Company is the leadership

Leadership requires a mix of professional knowledge and expertise along with the ability to develop and manage good systems to sustain quality teaching and learning. OGS has leadership which is:

  • More than Management
  • Involves forward planning, vision, foresight, school development planning, communication, student needs, general administration
  • Curriculum leader, financial and resource manager
  • Leadership style
  • Change Agent

The board’s role is to ensure that:

  • The school runs smoothly
  • quality programmes are in place.
  • school staff are employed including the principal
  • policies to guide the operation of the school are in place.
  • Handover plan to school
  • Periodic audits
  • Transition the knowledge
  • Operational and Strategic Planning

School as Teams within a Team

  • Organizing in teams for work distribution, management control, distribution of duties, information gathering and processing.
  • Working Together in Teams produces greater output.
  • Human Resources
  • Curriculum
  • Marketting
  • Communication
  • Resources and Finance
  • Discipline
  • Record Keeping
  • Guidance
