Why OGS

Recently, there has been a surge of interest and establishment of single-gender schools due to educational research

Educationists believe that there are persistent gender differences in how boys and girls learn and behave in educational settings, and that such differences merit educating them separately.

Studies show that brains of males and females develop differently.Due to these developmental differences and by separating students according to gender, the educator is able to meet the needs according to the developmental trajectory of the different genders. In addition, educationists argue that by segregating the genders, students do not become distracted by the other gender's actions in the classrooms, therefore making them pay attention more to class than their peers.

In Kashmir, of late there is a growing trend of Co ed Scools. But we need to embrace a new understanding that the world has shifted dramatically. The modern concept is gender differences in the ways girls and boys learn and behave merit educating them separately.

We are Beginning to See the Light


That is to orbit around Girls empowerment The need was felt of a All Girls School format here in the valley that shall create opportunities that don't exist in the coed classroom.
