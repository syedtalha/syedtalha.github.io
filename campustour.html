Campus Tour

Once an application has been received, all parents are invited to attend a comprehensive Introductory Session. We believe your daughter’s education is one of the most important decisions you will make. It is vital that you see the school and meet the people who will be part of her life and, because of this, attending this event is a compulsory part of the enrolment process. Our Introductory sessions provide an opportunity to learn about our educational programs, to meet our senior and specialist staff, to see the school and to meet our Principal to discuss your daughter’s enrolment.
